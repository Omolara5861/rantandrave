About Rant & Rave

Our portal gives you a platform to express your dissatisfaction or satisfaction about your organisation, government, local and public services, school or university, etc., with service delivery, an unfulfilled contract, purchase issues, unfair deductions, extortions, etc. It is also an avenue to call out and expose organisations, to alert people about a scam or a potential one, to whistle-blow, etc. It is also an avenue to inform people of good services and opportunities that may be available. Rant and Rave is a website where you can share your experience, be it a general experience, customer service, product delivery, workplace experiences, etc, It also serves as a website featuring local, national, or international news.
